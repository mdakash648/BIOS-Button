1. Create shortcuts button. [Mouse Right click > new > shortcut]
2. copy and paste this " C:\Windows\System32\shutdown.exe /r /t 0 /fw "
3. Type your shortcut name. example: Bios
4. click next
5. select this and click mouse right button.
6. click "properties" click "advanced" click "run as a administrator"
7. click ok ok ok...
8. double click bios shortcut button than click yes. your pc restart and open bios mode.
